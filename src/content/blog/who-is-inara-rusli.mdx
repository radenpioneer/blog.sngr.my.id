Inara Rusli is a celebrity in Indonesia. She was born on February 19, 1993. She is known for being the wife of Virgoun, a famous Indonesian musician. She was also a former member of the girl band Bexxa. Recently, she has been in the news for her decision to remove her veil and for her fashion style .
